In an age of heightened awareness regarding health and climate change, consumers are increasingly seeking transparency in the food they purchase. The modern grocery store environment is saturated with complex labels, marketing jargon, and ambiguous environmental claims that make it difficult to quickly and accurately assess a product's nutritional value and ecological impact. The challenge lies in providing immediate, understandable, and actionable data at the point of purchase. People want to know: "Is this healthy for me?" and "Is this good for the planet?" without needing a degree in nutrition science or environmental policy.

This lack of transparency often leads to uninformed choices that may negatively impact personal health and contribute to unsustainable consumption patterns.


üí° Solution

The Sustainable Food Tracker is a powerful web and mobile application designed to bridge this information gap. By leveraging the comprehensive, community-driven database of the OpenFoodFacts API, our platform transforms complex data into simple, digestible insights.

Users can instantly scan a product's barcode or enter it manually to retrieve vital information:

Nutritional Health Grade: A clear indicator of how healthy the product is.

Eco-Score: A transparent view of the product's environmental footprint.

Additive & Allergen Alerts: Warnings about specific ingredients.

CO‚ÇÇ Impact Assessment: An evaluation of the product's contribution to climate change.

We provide clarity in an opaque industry, empowering users to make educated, sustainable choices in real-time.

‚ú® Features

The Sustainable Food Tracker is packed with features designed for ease of use and maximum impact:

Core Functionality
Input: Use manual input to find product information in seconds.

Clear Grading System: Intuitive, color-coded letter grades (A-E) for both Nutri-Score (health) and Eco-Score (environment).

Detailed Nutrition Facts: A clean visualization of macronutrients, vitamins, and minerals per 100g, powered by Chart.js for easy comparison.

Additive and Allergen Alerts: Immediate flags for potentially harmful additives (E-numbers) and common allergens.

Sustainable Alternatives: When a product scores poorly, the app suggests healthier and greener alternatives from the same category.

Gamification & Engagement

"Eco-Points" System: Earn points for scanning products with good environmental scores.

User Profile & Achievements: Track your progress and unlock virtual badges  as you make better choices.

Personal Scan History: A food diary to review past choices and monitor your long-term consumption habits.

Leaderboard: Compete with friends or the community to see who is making the most sustainable choices, fostering a healthy competition for a better planet.
